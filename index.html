About Me



Hi, I am a Los-Angeles based artist. I practice different types of art, ranging from digital to traditional. I graduated with a BA in illustration at Art Center College of Design. I have been working as an art director and a sketch artist and illustrator at various advertising agencies.

I am taking this coding class to boost my skills in creative softwares. The codes I learn for web development will also add to my toolbox for my advertising career as well.

I have attended many art schools after I graduated from Art Center to practice different types of art that are within the entertainment field and marketing for entertainment. I find web development a crucial component of marketing as technology has transformed the means of the print media to digital media. That is why I am taking this class.

I am very happy with what I have learned so far, and I can see the possibilities of the application ofgit what I am learning from this class already.
This is very exciting and I look forward to learning more!
